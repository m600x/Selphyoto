Scaling

Calibration guides are 50mm.

New scaling = current × (expected ÷ measured).

E.g. if scaling is 0.961 and you measure 52 mm instead of 50: 0.961 × (50 ÷ 52) ≈ 0.924.

Instructions

Send the exported image to your smartphone and load it into the Selphy app. Make sure that the printing setting is set to "borderless printing".
